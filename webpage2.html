THE AIR CURTAIN MANUFACTURERS

Looking for cost effective solutions to provide a clean and safe environment at your work place? Air curtains are known to be the perfect answers to the problem. These are simple mechanical devices that aid in keeping your work place hygienic and dust free. We at Airbeam are known to specialize in the air handling technology catering to the needs of a wide range of clients not only in Chennai but pan India and abroad.

Airbeam is one of the fastest growing Air curtain manufacturers in Chennai with an impressive clientele. Founded in the year 1998 we have been providing cutting edge solutions to maintain a healthy and clean work environment. The company is known for a wide range of international quality products that are not only cost effective but energy efficient. Apart from our flagship product which is the air curtains, we also specialize in a host of other products. The list includes PVC Strip Curtains, Shoe Polishing and Shinning Machines, Hand Dryers, Fly Killing Machines, paper towel dispensers and many more.

Airbeam has its own production unit that adheres to the highest quality standards in manufacturing. The company apart from manufacturing also deals in installation and service as well. We have a dedicated team of 30 technical and non technical experts working round the clock to provide 100% customer satisfaction in terms of quality and cost.
